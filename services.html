OUR SERVICES

We can provide personalized services to help you achieve the look you want and feel comfortable with

Haircut

Begins with a consultation between the client and our hairstylist to discuss the desired haircut style, length, and preferences. Whether you're looking for a simple trim or a complete style overhaul, our skilled barber can help you achieve the look you want.

Shaving

Our barbers will use specialized tools, such as scissors or clippers, to cut and shave the hair according to the agreed-upon style. They may also use other tools, such as razors or trimmers, to achieve a more precise or detailed cut.

Pedicure

treatment for your feet and toenails which involves soaking your feet in warm water, exfoliating the skin, shaping and trimming the toenails, and applying other decorative elements

Manicure

Having nails cleaned, buffed, and polished to enhance their appearance. The cuticles may be pushed back or trimmed, and the hands will be moisturized with lotions or oils to soften the skin

Facials

Skin treatment that involves cleansing, exfoliating, and nourishing the skin on the face by our skincare professionals. They use a variety of products and techniques tailored to the individual's skin type and concerns. During your facial, the skin is typically cleansed with a gentle cleanser or exfoliated with an exfoliating scrub. A mask or other treatment will be applied to the skin to nourish and hydrate it, and extractions will be performed to remove any blackheads or other impurities. Finally, a moisturizer or other finishing product is typically applied to leave the skin feeling soft and refreshed.

Dreadlock

We understand that dreadlock styling requires regular maintenance to keep the hair healthy and prevent breakage or damage. Our methods are regular washing, conditioning, and moisturizing, as well as retwisting or tightening the locks as needed. Our Professional maintenance and styling services are available from licensed stylists .

Pool

Our thoughtfully designed gaming area boasts a professional-grade pool table and a vibrant ambiance that exudes both sophistication and fun. It's the perfect setting to relax, socialize, and enjoy a friendly game of billiards while getting a fresh haircut or waiting for your turn in the barber chair.
